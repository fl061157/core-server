About ChatGame

ChatGame is a free video call app that supports video/voice call, voice/text message and photo sharing on all major smartphones. You can now connect with your friends at anytime, anywhere you want with wifi/3G/4G connection. Simply log in with your phone number, and ChatGame will recognize your contacts who are already ChatGame users. Additionally, you can add & chat with other ChatGame users by searching for their phone numbers.

Free to use

With Wifi/3G/4G connection, communicate anywhere, any way.

Free video/voice calls, voice/text messaging & cute emojis.

Video/voice calls do not incur mobile call costs.

Text messages do not incur mobile text costs.

Connect with the world easily

Easy registration. simple UI, clear function, ChatGame has it all.

Auto-detect ChatGame users from phone contacts.

Never miss a call/message, even when you go offline. Receive notification the next time you log on.

Set favorite contacts so you don’t look it up next time.

Hidden Fun

High quality video calling technology guarantees a good time between you & your friends.

Play mini-games during waiting time. (iOS)
